How It Works?

Choose a Trail & Checkout

Choose from our selection of trails in the city you love. Add your team details on the checkout page.

You’ve Got Mail

Each team captain receives the trail instructions and unique play links for all players. Team captains then forward this email to the other team members.

Start

Head to the start location and click play to start your adventure. It’s quick and easy with no downloads required.

Enjoy…

Have fun as you solve clues and discover the best parts of the city!

The Gameplay

1 Use Hints

When clues get a little tricky use a hint. Watch out though as points will be deducted for each hint used.

2 Solve Clues

Think like as a wizard as you and your team try to solve the cryptic clues

3 Score points

Score points with each successful answer and challenge for the top spot on the leaderboard!

4 Explore & Learn

Immerse yourself in the history of the city as you explore the least walked paths and learn the incredible backstories. Cool images and Videos thrown in to add to the fun experience.

After The Trail, The Fun Doesn't End

Check out your stats

Each team is provided with their detailed stats after the trail. See your answers to the clues and any hints you may have used in easy to read graph format.

Read our Ultimate Guide

Interested to learn even more about the places you visited along the way? Then make sure to read the ultimate guide. Our experts have compiled this list which includes the best places to visit and things to do in the city.

Leaderboard

Your teams’ points will be uploaded to the adventure clues leaderboard. See how you compare against all the other teams.
