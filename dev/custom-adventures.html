What You Can Expect

  • Solve Custom Clues

    Split up into your teams that will be decided before hand. All teams simply click on their play links to begin. Get ready to think like as a wizard as you and your team try to solve the cryptic clues and go on your custom adventure.

  • Hints

    When clues get a little tricky use a hint. Watch out though as points will be deducted for each hint used

  • Score Points

  • Custom Contect

    As you Immerse yourself in the adventure why not throw in some clues or special memories about who’s birthday it is, or cool facts interlinked to your company and team members. Past events have included videos of the CEO delivering clues on video. Think outside the box – Custom Content is up to you!
